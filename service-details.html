Making critical systems faster, safer, and easier to change

Vionix Consulting focuses on existing software that has become slow, fragile, or hard to evolve. Rather than proposing risky big-bang rewrites, we help you understand how your systems behave today and make targeted improvements that reduce risk and improve performance.

  • Database and reporting performance reviews that cut runtimes and operational friction.
  • Stabilisation and refactoring of legacy modules and services that are currently seen as too risky to change.
  • Modernisation paths that align architecture, development workflow, and incremental delivery.
  • Selective use of AI to remove repetitive work for developers and operations teams.

The aim is to leave you with systems that are easier to reason about, faster under load, and safer to change—without discarding the value already invested in your existing software.

Each engagement is scoped around a concrete problem, such as a slow reporting workload or a fragile core service, and delivered as a focused assessment or rescue sprint with clear outcomes.

Database & Reporting Performance

We focus on the queries, reports, and batch jobs that slow your business down.

  • Review schemas, indexes, and query patterns to identify real bottlenecks.
  • Optimise critical reports and jobs so they complete within acceptable time windows.
  • Separate operational and analytical workloads where needed to avoid contention.
  • Introduce lightweight observability around key queries and jobs to catch regressions early.

Legacy Core Stabilisation & Refactoring

We help teams regain control over complex, business-critical modules and services.

  • Map responsibilities and data flows to expose hidden coupling and risks.
  • Refactor hot spots into clearer structures and boundaries without wholesale rewrites.
  • Add tests and verification steps around critical behaviours to make future changes safer.
  • Document the resulting module so new team members can work effectively and confidently.

Incremental System Modernisation & Team Enablement

Modernisation only works if architecture and day-to-day development reinforce each other.

  • Define realistic target architecture slices and incremental system evolution paths.
  • Align branching, reviews, and CI so code flow supports the architecture.
  • Introduce lightweight quality gates (tests, static analysis, review checklists) that fit the team size.
  • Run focused architecture or code-review sessions using your real codebase.

AI-Assisted Workflows

We introduce AI where it clearly makes your technical workflows more efficient, not as an end in itself.

  • Identify developer and operations tasks that benefit from summarisation or automation.
  • Prototype assistants for documentation search, log triage, or repetitive boilerplate.
  • Integrate assistants with existing tools and services using established APIs.
  • Define guardrails so AI use remains safe, auditable, and reversible.
