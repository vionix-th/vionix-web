Expert Software Consulting & Training

Vionix Consulting provides specialized services in software architecture, code reviews, and AI integration, helping organizations build robust, maintainable, and secure software solutions.

Comprehensive Software Solutions for Modern and Legacy Systems

We specialize in designing scalable, maintainable architectures and conducting thorough code reviews for both modern and legacy systems. Our expertise spans multiple programming languages including C, C++, Java, and VisualBasic6, allowing us to provide tailored solutions for any technology stack.

  • Expert software architecture design and review for optimal performance and security
  • Professional code refactoring and modernization across any language or paradigm
  • Seamless AI/LLM integration for enhanced functionality and automation

Our modernization strategies focus on API integration and modularization, helping organizations transform their systems without complete rewrites. We develop REST, SOAP, or custom APIs to connect legacy or modern systems to new platforms, ensuring smooth integration and future scalability.

In addition to technical consulting, we offer comprehensive training programs including workshops on software architecture principles, code review best practices, and AI/LLM integration strategies. Our training is available both remotely and on-site, with options for custom team training and online course access.

We serve organizations across multiple sectors in Thailand, Germany, and the USA, including finance, manufacturing, healthcare, and government. Our unique combination of legacy system knowledge and modern development practices makes us the ideal partner for organizations looking to modernize their software infrastructure while maintaining operational continuity.
